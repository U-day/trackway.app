Location Use

We will use your ‘live’ Location information to share with others on your trip only while the Live Tracking Map screen is open. This is to inform all those on your trip as to each other’s location. This information may be used to alert others in your group or a designated person(s) in case of any likely or anticipated ‘incident’.

In addition, your ‘past’ Location information will be used to show past paths to the those on your trip. This information may also be used to develop and present other ride related statistics to you and your group.
